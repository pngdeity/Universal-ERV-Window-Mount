# Concept: Modular Telescoping Window Mount

## 1. System Overview
The system consists of a central **Core Plate** and two or more **Extension Sliders**. This allows one design to be printed or manufactured once and adjusted to fit windows from 400mm to 1200mm.

## 2. Key Components

### A. The Core Plate (`core_plate.stl`)
- **Function**: Primary structural member.
- **Features**: 
    - 162mm aperture (for standard 160mm pipes).
    - Integrated mounting points for a **Cantilever Brace**.
    - "Tongue and Groove" edges to accept Extension Sliders.
    - Deep channels for 10mm EPDM foam tape.

### B. Extension Sliders (`extension_slider.stl`)
- **Function**: Adapts the width of the assembly.
- **Features**: 
    - Slotted bolt holes for fine-tuning width.
    - Designed to be "nested" for compact 3D printing.

### C. The Universal Pipe Collar (`pipe_collar.stl`)
- **Function**: Grips the ERV pipe and prevents it from sliding in/out.
- **Features**: 
    - Two-piece bolt-together design.
    - Internal ridges to "bite" into the SDR35 PVC or commercial ERV plastic tube.

### D. Sill-Support Brace (`sill_support.stl`)
- **Function**: Transfers torque to the window sill.
- **Features**: 
    - Adjustable angle to account for different sill slopes.
    - Large "foot" to distribute pressure and avoid damaging the window frame.

## 3. Sealing Strategy
- **Interface Seal**: V-groove track for 1/2" Silicone bulb seals.
- **Panel Joinery**: Lap joints between Core and Extension panels to prevent light and air bypass.
