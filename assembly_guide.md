# Assembly Guide: Pioneer ECOasis 50 Window Mount

This guide details the assembly and installation of the universal window mount system for the **Pioneer ECOasis 50 (ERV-50)**.

## 1. Printing Requirements
Ensure all parts are printed in **ASA** or **PETG** for UV and heat resistance.
- **1x Core Plate** (`pioneer_core_plate.stl`)
- **2x Extension Sliders** (`pioneer_extension_slider.stl`)
- **1x Sill Support** (`pioneer_sill_support.stl`)
- **2x Pipe Collar Halves** (Generated from OpenSCAD if needed)

## 2. Hardware Required
- 8x M5 x 25mm Bolts & Wing Nuts (For Slider adjustment)
- 1x M6 x 100mm Bolt & Nut (For Sill Support pivot)
- 4x Self-tapping screws (For Pioneer Metal Plate to Core Plate)
- 2 meters of 1/2" Silicone Bulb Seal
- 1 roll of 10mm EPDM Foam Tape

## 3. Assembly Steps

### Step 1: Panel Preparation
1.  Clean the "Tongue and Groove" edges of the `Core Plate` and `Extension Sliders`.
2.  Apply a strip of **10mm EPDM Foam Tape** inside the grooves of the Core Plate to ensure an airtight joint between panels.

### Step 2: Width Adjustment
1.  Slide the two `Extension Sliders` into the `Core Plate`.
2.  Place the assembly in your window opening and expand the sliders until they touch the window jambs.
3.  Secure the sliders in place using the **M5 Bolts and Wing Nuts** through the integrated slots.

### Step 3: Installing the Pioneer Mounting Plate
1.  Take the metal mounting plate included with your **Pioneer ECOasis 50**.
2.  Align the plate with the **Safe-Drill Zones** (indicated by the slots/markings on the Core Plate).
3.  Drive self-tapping screws through the metal plate into the Core Plate. The 20mm thickness provides ample "bite" for the screws.

### Step 4: Weather Stripping
1.  Apply the **Silicone Bulb Seal** around the entire outer perimeter of the assembled panels (Top, Bottom, and both sides).
2.  This seal will compress against the window track to prevent air bypass.

### Step 5: Final Installation
1.  Lift the assembly into the window and close the window sash onto the top edge of the mount.
2.  Install the **Sill Support** brace from the outside. Pivot the arm until it rests firmly on the window sill and lock the M6 bolt.
3.  Slide your Pioneer ERV through the 172mm aperture and hook it onto the metal plate.
4.  Ensure the pipe has a **1-degree downward slope** toward the outside for drainage.
