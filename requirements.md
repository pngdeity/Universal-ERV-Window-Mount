# Design Requirements: Universal Window Adapter

## 1. Dimensional Compatibility
*   **Target Pipe Diameters**: 100mm (4") and 160mm (6.3").
*   **Window Types**: Vertical Single/Double Hung and Horizontal Sliding.
*   **Adjustability Range**: Must accommodate window widths/heights from 400mm to 1200mm.

## 2. Structural Integrity
*   **Cantilever Support**: Must support a weight of at least 3kg (ERV + Core + Fans) without visible sagging or stress at the window track.
*   **Material Fatigue**: Clamping mechanisms must be designed for ASA or Aluminum to prevent "creep" under compression.

## 3. Thermal & Air Sealing
*   **Thermal Break**: The adapter plate must have a R-value > 3.0 (e.g., using rigid EPS/XPS foam core or double-walled plastic with insulation).
*   **Air Leakage**: Must achieve near-zero leakage between the adapter and the window track using EPDM or Silicone bulb seals.
*   **Condensation Management**: Must mandate or include a 1-2 degree outward pitch to ensure water drainage.

## 4. Aesthetics & Ergonomics
*   **Tool-Less Install**: Ideally, the unit should be secured using hand-tightened wing nuts or spring-loaded plungers.
*   **Visibility**: Design should minimize blocked light (e.g., placing the unit at the extreme edge of the window).
